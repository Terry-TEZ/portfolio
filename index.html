About

I am an Artist/Designer from Otepoti Dunedin, I have been creating art for as long as I can remember. I currently work mostly in digital programs such as Clip Studio Paint and Adobe Photoshop as well as traditionally. My work commonly depicts figures and franchises that I am a fan of, whether that be musicians or films/TV, as well as original designs and characters. I am currently Studying at Otago Polytechnic in a Digital Design Certificate.
